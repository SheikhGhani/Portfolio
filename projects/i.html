Ingenius Prep

Ingenius Prep is a prominent education consultancy based in the USA, specializing in providing comprehensive services for college and graduate school admissions. The company offers strategic guidance and support to students in navigating the complex application processes, including test preparation, essay editing, and overall application strategy. Ingenius Prep aims to empower students to achieve their academic and professional goals by leveraging a team of experienced counselors and educators.

As a Senior DevOps Engineer at Ingenius Prep, I played a crucial role in enhancing the efficiency of the organization's software development lifecycle. My responsibilities included the development and management of AWS cloud environments, the implementation of CI/CD pipelines using AWS CodePipeline and Jenkins, and the strategic containerization of the application using Docker. This approach aimed to improve scalability, reliability, and deployment efficiency. Additionally, I led the implementation of robust monitoring and alarm systems using AWS CloudWatch, ensuring the seamless performance of the production environment, which serves a substantial user base of counselors and students.
